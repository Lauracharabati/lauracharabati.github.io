Long summer skirts

January 10, 2021
























Long flowy skirts are a must have for summer time! This purple skirt combined with red flowers is beautiful and feminine, and offers a slight bohemian twist. I 100% recommend this skirt for a warm summer's day as it is made from super lightweight and breathable material, making it cool and comfortable. The slit in the skirt will also help to keep the wearer cool, and adds an interesting and appealing aspect to the design. Dominique paired this skirt with a white off the shoulder crop top, and styled it with the currently very popular gold boho jewellery.

After a very long eight hour drive up the coast, we arrived at the beautiful Cabarita Beach for our week holiday. This was a perfect outfit that Dominique wore when on our holiday. We spent this day exploring the seaside, soaking up the sun and admiring the lovely views. During our adventure, we discovered a very long walking trail up a hill which gave us an amazing view of the entire beach.
