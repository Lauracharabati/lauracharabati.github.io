Simple but cute outfits

March 1, 2021

























A simple summer outfit can be dressed up, by embracing colour and adding a slight twist to the style. A top and shorts can be dressed up and made stylish, by adding your own flair to it! This simple yellow top has been made more interesting with the off the shoulder design and the bright and vibrant colour, making the outfit pop. I love the off the shoulder style as it elongates the neck and creates a look that is both fun and intriguing. Jasmine paired this top with the currently very trendy dark blue denim shorts. The white rips on both sides of the front of the pants add a cool, youthful touch to this outfit without having to actually dress down this look. For her shoes, Jasmine wore a pair of white Nike Air Forces which add a fresh, clean look. The purple crystal necklace that she wore is very pretty and feminine, and completes the outfit.
