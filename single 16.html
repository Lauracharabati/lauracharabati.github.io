Red floral

August 12, 2021
























This outfit is hands down one of my favourite outfits yet! I believe floral pattern is an absolutely essential way to spice up an outfit and is a must have pattern. I love that this outfit is cute, pretty and girly, while the high topped Converses add a slight edgy touch to the look. I love the combination of the white flowers against the red, and the layers of frills on the skirt as they add a nice feminine look.
