Staying stylish in lockdown

July 10, 2021

























Staying stylish while in lockdown is very important for fashion obsessed people like myself! I have recently enjoyed indulging in a bit of online shopping during this lockdown. The top, jeans and shoes that Dominique is wearing are new purchases I have recently made, which I asked her to model for this photo shoot. I am currently loving blue denim ripped jeans for winter (even though we may be stuck in lockdown for the rest of winter!). Even though I will not be going anywhere fancy, at least I will be able to wear some of my new fashion on my daily walks. Today we went for a walk by the Harbour Bridge at the very lovely Milsons Point. Despite it being incredibly windy, the sun was out, making it a nice day.
