Soft shades of purple

April 10, 2021

























As you may be able to tell from this dress I recently purchased from Tigerlily, I am all about soft and light purple shades for summer. I love the muted tones of lilac, lavender, mauve, white and the slight tint of dusty pink throughout this dress. These colours work beautifully together and are great for pastel lovers. With the different shades and tones, this dress is such a statement piece and is very eye catching.
