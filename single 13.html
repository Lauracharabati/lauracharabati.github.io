Parisian vintage

May 31, 2021
























Today Aria and I went to the lovely French cafe, Café de la Fontaine, for lunch. I would highly recommend visiting this cafe! It is an experience that will make you feel like you are actually in France! The cafe has a very delicious menu of freshly-made eclairs, authentic crepes, galettes, quiches, baguettes and ice cream. I would also suggest trying out the coffee, which I rate higly! The interior decoration of the café is authentic Parisian, decorated with white orchids, hanging antique glass & brass lanterns, bejeweled chandeliers and soft Dior grey wood paneling. Inside the cafe in the corner of the room, there was a pretty lit up wire Eiffel tower which added to the French experience. For today's outfit, Aria wore a white collar shirt under a grey Assembly Label jumper, paired with a vintage white and black plaid skirt. She finished off this outfit with a pair of black sunglasses and a brown baguette bag.
























OUTFIT DETAILS:
Jumper: Glassons
Collar shirt: Cotton On
Skirt, sunglasses and handbag: Princess Polly
Shoes: Nike
