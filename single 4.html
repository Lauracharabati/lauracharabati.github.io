Butterfly farm

January 13, 2021
























Today we took a mini roadtrip from Cabarita Beach to Byron Bay and spent part of the day at Byron's famous farm. The blue skies and sunshine were out while we explored the beautiful, luscious grassy fields and admired the sunflowers. Amongst the sunflowers, we were greeted by a group of pretty butterflies and one flew onto Dominique's hand! For lunch we ate at the Three Blue Ducks cafe at The Farm, chooosing from their delicious menu based on ethically farmed produce and authentic food. For today's outfit, Dominique wore a loose white crop top, purple wide-legged, flowy pants and beige criss cross sandals. This summer boho outfit was appropriate for the laid-back, hippie atmosphere of Byron Bay. Dominique styled this outfit with a Tiffany & Co. blue and silver double heart necklace, which complemented her outfit. This added an elegant and feminine touch to the look and completed her final outfit.

Top: Glassons

Necklace: Tiffany & Co.
