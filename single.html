Garden fairy

January 2, 2021

















Summer time is for wearing fun dresses and exploring and discovering new beautiful locations! Today Ivy and I visited one of the many beautiful gardens in Sydney, the exquisite Vaucluse House, and admired its luscious gardens and pretty little cottage. This pretty, light green, polka dot summer dress looked perfect when worn with a pair of white birkenstocks. The fabric of the dress is thin, lightweight and breathable, making it perfect to wear on a hot summer's day as it will not stick to your skin no matter how hot it gets! Ivy topped off this outfit very nicely with a pair of sunglasses and gold jewellery which is very on trend at the moment. She wore a gold double necklace, rings and two earrings, which complimented this outfit well. The sunglasses and jewellery were the main stand out pieces of this outfit. This simple yet still cute and feminine summer look is perfect for teenage girls of any age who enjoy dressing up!
