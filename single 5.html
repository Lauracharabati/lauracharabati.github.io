Boho finds

January 16, 2021
























Discovering afforable bohemian styled clothes has always been a major interest of mine. I love to explore and discover new boho shops, experiment with different patterns and mix and match clothing items. From my discovery over the years, some of my favourite bohemian brands to shop at include:
1. Spell and The Gypsy
2. Tree of Life
3. Tigerlily
4. Black Boho
5. Boho Buys
6. White Bohemian

I would very much recommend all of these brands to my boho lovers. My two favourite brands from this list however are Tree of Life and Tigerlily. Dominique's outfit on this day included boho clothes and accessories I have previously bought from a few different brands. I love the pretty patterns, detail, and the combination of exotic colours on these pants, which definitely make these pants eye catching and a main stand out piece of this outfit. The white, off the shoulder, frilly top is one of my personal favourite items in my wardrobe. It dresses up this outfit while still achieving a simple, relaxed and comfortable look, which is an essential aspect of the boho style. Dominique styled this outfit with a gold headpiece and feather earrings, which are the perfect boho accessories if you ask me! These accessories add a sparkle and help to amp up the outfit.






















OUTFIT/ITEMS DETAILS:
Top: White Bohemian
Pants: Tree of Life
Shoes: Ruby
Headpiece and earrings: Sportsgirl
