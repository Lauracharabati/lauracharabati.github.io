Off the shoulder tops

April 16, 2021

























Am I the only one that never understood why off the shoulder tops went out of trend? I am so glad these tops are coming back in trend in 2021, and they definitely should of never lost their flair. These tops show off your shoulders and elongate your neck, which creates a look which is very stylish and flattering. Off the shoulder tops also allow you to keep cool on warm days, which will be very ideal on a hot summer's day. These tops are very feminine and are perfect for teenage girls of any age who like to dress up!
