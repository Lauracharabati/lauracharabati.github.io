Valentine's Day outfit inspo

February 12, 2021

























Hello there! As we know Valentine's Day is right around the corner, which means time to start outfit planning. I have put together a very feminine and modist outfit worn by Ivy above, which is the ultimate outfit for a Valentine's Day picnic. This super cute, light green, polka dot sun dress from Glassons is the perfect fashion item for this occassion. The dress is very comfortable, light and airy as well as chic and fashionable. Ivy matched this with a white cardigan from Seed, which she effortlessly wore off the shoulders. For her jewellery, Ivy wore a gold double necklace, hoop earrings and rings, to add a bit of sparkle to this look. She wore her hair in a half up, half down style, which is the perfect combination of casual, effortless and chic. That completed this casual, yet still cute and fashionable outfit. I hope this provided you with a great source of inspiration for your Valentine's Day outfit, please let me know what you think! xo
