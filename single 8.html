Polka dots

February 28, 2021

























Polka dots are a fashion trend that never go out of style. Polka dots have been in trend since the fifties, and are not going out of trend anytime soon. Not a summer goes by where the humble polka dots have not made a must-try trend. An advantage of polka dots is that you can literally wear them for any occassion, from the most casual days to the swishest of events. There are a million ways to wear polka dots, enabling you to create countless looks for each season. Polka dots are a totally essential way to dress up an outfit, as they add a cute and fun touch to the look.

Dominique put together this perfect summer look with her maroon and white polka dot crop top and navy denim shorts. The white dots stand out very nicely against the maroon, creating a main point of interest in this look. These sweet yet sophisticated polka dots bring this outfit to life and step up the look. Dominique has worn a sparkly gold and silver necklace, which is not the most noticeable aspect of this outfit as the polka dots are the most prominent feature that attract the most attention.
